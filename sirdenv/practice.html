Welcome to the Mario Fan Site!

Here you'll find information about everyone's favorite plumber-turned-hero, Mario! Most of the information here are just the basics of the Mario franchise. A beginner's introduction if you will. But let's be honest, who still doesn't know Mario nowadays?

About Mario

Mario is a fictional character created by Japanese video game designer Shigeru Miyamoto. He is the main character and mascot of Nintendo's Mario franchise.

Some key facts about Mario:

  • First appeared in the arcade game Donkey Kong in 1981
  • He's known for his red cap, blue overalls, and mustache.
  • He is a love interest for Princess Peach.
  • Has a brother named Luigi (who is often overshadowed by Mario).
  • Stars in various game genres not limited to platformers!

Mario's Crew

These are his friends that are often depicted with him in media!

Luigi

Mario's younger brother, often depicted as a coward.

Princess Peach

The ruler of the Mushroom Kingdom, often taken by Bowser.

Yoshi

A friendly dinosaur (he found Baby Mario and helped reunite the brothers).

Toad

A loyal servant of Princess Peach and a helpful ally to Mario.

Princess Daisy

Princess Daisy is the princess of Sarasaland.

Donkey Kong

Donkey Kong is a powerful ape who is both an ally and sometimes a rival to Mario. He loves bananas.

Rosalina

Rosalina is the guardian of the cosmos and the adoptive mother of the Lumas. She first appeared in Super Mario Galaxy.

Wario

Wario is Mario's greedy rival. Has a love for garlic.

Waluigi

Waluigi is Luigi's rival and often teams up with Wario.

Toadette

Toadette is a cheerful and adventurous Toad who often accompanies Toad on various adventures.

Mario's Enemies

Of course, what's an adventure without enemies? Mario has tons of them but let's bring out his most known enemies:

Bowser

He often kidnaps Princess Peach and has a son named Bowser Jr. (he's a good dad though).

Goomba

Goombas are common enemies in the Mario series, often found in large numbers.

Bowser Jr.

Bowser Jr. is Bowser's son and often assists his father in kidnapping Princess Peach and battling Mario.

King Boo

King Boo is the leader of the Boos and a major antagonist in the Luigi's Mansion series.

Wart

Wart is the main antagonist of Super Mario Bros. 2, known for his ability to spit bubbles.

Chain Chomp

Chain Chomps are large, ball-and-chain enemies that lunge at Mario when he gets too close.

Piranha Plant

Piranha Plants are carnivorous plants that emerge from pipes and try to bite Mario.

Shy Guy

Shy Guys are masked enemies that come in various colors and often carry spears or other weapons.
